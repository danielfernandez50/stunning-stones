About Us

New Company, Seasoned Hands

Stunning Stones is a new company built on years of hands-on experience in stone fabrication and installation. Our team has spent years perfecting the craft — working with marble, quartz, granite, and quartzite across kitchens, bathrooms, and custom projects throughout Central Florida.

We started Stunning Stones to do things the right way: premium materials, precision fabrication, and the kind of personal attention that larger shops can't offer. Every slab we touch gets the same dedication to detail, whether it's your first countertop or a full kitchen transformation.

Stone Selection

Materials We Work With

We source the finest natural and engineered stones from around the world. Visit our showroom or browse our suppliers to find the perfect slab for your project.

Marble

Timeless elegance with unique veining. Calacatta, Carrara, Statuario, and exotic varieties.

Quartz

Engineered for durability and consistency. Low maintenance, stain-resistant, with a wide range of colors.

Granite

Natural strength and beauty. Heat-resistant and incredibly durable for high-traffic kitchens.

Quartzite

The best of both worlds: natural stone beauty with exceptional hardness and heat resistance.
